What DSW Does Differently

Design. Build. Maintain. Own It.

DSW handles the full scope—design and planning, installation, irrigation, and seasonal maintenance—as one integrated process, not four disconnected vendors.

  • Dustin on every project. Not just the proposal—the planning, the build, and the walkthrough. Every decision runs through the person whose name is on the company.

  • Quotes that hold. Final cost comes in right at the number quoted—no under-bidding to win the job, no adding line items once work begins.

  • Transparent communication. Slowdowns get addressed immediately—not buried or explained away after the fact. You're never left wondering what's happening.

  • Work built to last. Quality takes planning, working the plan, and adjusting along the way. DSW builds yards that hold up through Colorado winters, not just through October.

The Process

How a DSW Project Works

Three straightforward steps. No handoffs, no surprises, no disappearing act once the contract is signed.

1

Free On-Site Estimate

Dustin visits your property, walks the full yard, and listens before he talks. He'll ask about how you use the space, what's not working, and what you'd love it to be. The estimate comes with a real plan, not a ballpark.

2

Design, Approve, Build

Once you've approved the plan and pricing, DSW's experienced crew gets to work—with Dustin on-site directing the job. Adjustments are communicated in real time, not buried in change orders.

3

Final Walkthrough & Ongoing Care

When the project wraps, Dustin walks the yard with you, explains the irrigation setup, and answers every question. For clients who want it, DSW handles ongoing seasonal maintenance so the investment holds.
