About Kelsey

One person. Full attention. Every time.

Kelsey Gabrielsen opened Beauty by KG in Parker, Colorado in 2025 — a deliberate decision to build something rare: a one-woman salon where you're not one of eight appointments that day. You're the appointment.

She sources products from The Refillary, a local Colorado company, because the quality shows and she won't compromise on it. She's hosted community mixers and supports other women-owned businesses in Parker. She's a small business owner managing family and a full client roster, and she brings that same care — efficient, warm, detail-oriented — to every chair.
