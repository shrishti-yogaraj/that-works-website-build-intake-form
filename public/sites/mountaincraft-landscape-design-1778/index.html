The Challenge

You built the house. Now you're staring at dirt.

New construction gives you a home. It doesn't give you a yard. And right now, you're facing three questions that don't have easy answers.

◻

"What's even possible here?"

You see a flat lot of compacted soil. You know it could be something — a patio, a fire pit, a place your kids actually want to play — but you can't picture it. You need someone who sees the potential in bare ground and knows how to unlock it.

◎

"Will it actually look like the plan?"

You've heard the stories. Beautiful sketches that become mediocre installs. Promises that erode the moment the contract is signed. You want to see exactly what you're getting before a single shovel touches dirt — and you want it to match.

△

"Who do I trust with this?"

Everyone claims to be the best. Few answer the phone after the job is done. You want a professional who listens, collaborates with you on the design, respects your budget, and doesn't disappear the moment the last stone is placed.

The Answer

Landscape design built on obsessive attention to detail.

MountainCraft doesn't mass-produce designs. Every project starts with your vision, your budget, and the unique character of your property. Ron handles design and installation personally — nothing is handed off to a crew you've never met.

  • ✦3D renderings before groundbreaking. Not sketches on a napkin. Full 3D models so you see exactly what your finished yard looks like before we start.
  • ✦Hand-selected materials. If the local supply doesn't meet the standard, Ron drives to the quarry and picks the right rocks himself. Every stone earns its place.
  • ✦Water features that look born, not built. Mountain streams, not prefab fountains. Every boulder placed to create natural flow and sound.
  • ✦Fast, focused execution. Most projects complete in under two weeks. Clean worksite every day. No drawn-out timelines.
  • ✦The relationship doesn't end at final payment. Questions after the project? Ron answers. That's not a policy — it's just how he works.

How It Works

From dirt to done. Three clear steps.

No mystery. No surprises. You'll know exactly what's happening and why at every stage.

1

Walk the Property Together

Ron meets you on site. You talk about how you want to live in this space — not just how you want it to look. He listens, takes notes, and starts seeing what your lot can become.

2

See It Before It's Built

Ron creates a detailed 3D design based on your input and budget. You review it together, refine it, and don't move forward until you're confident in every element. No guessing.

3

Watch It Come to Life

Installation moves fast and clean. Ron personally oversees every detail — hand-placing rocks, grading slopes, positioning plants. Most projects wrap in under two weeks.

About Ron

One person. One standard. No compromises.

MountainCraft is a one-man design operation based in Parker, Colorado. Ron is the landscape architect, the designer, and the guy placing the rocks. There's no sales team, no project manager buffer, no hand-off to a crew you've never met.

Since 2015, he's worked exclusively with homeowners in the Denver suburbs who care about getting their outdoor space right. Not the cheapest option. Not the fastest option. The right option.

Ron's process is simple: listen carefully, design in 3D so there are no surprises, source the best materials even when that means driving to the quarry himself, and install everything with a level of care that makes the finished space look like it was always there.
